## Overview

Your **Dashboard** is a comprehensive view of your career development through events. It tracks:

- **Goal Progress**: How events align with your career objectives
- **Skill Growth**: Technical and soft skills learned from attended events
- **Networking Insights**: Connections made and network expansion
- **Event Timeline**: Visual history of your event attendance
- **Career Impact Trends**: How your career-relevant event attendance has evolved

![Dashboard Overview](/images/dashboard-overview.png)

## Dashboard Layout

### Desktop View

The dashboard uses a **responsive grid layout** with cards:

- **Header Section**: Welcome message, quick stats
- **Focus Hero Card**: Your top priority goal with progress
- **Analytics Grid**: 2-3 column layout with metric cards
- **Timeline Section**: Chronological event history
- **Insights Panel**: AI-generated career insights (coming soon)

### Mobile View

On mobile, cards stack vertically in order of priority:

1. Focus Hero Card
2. Career Progress
3. Learning Progress
4. Event Pipeline
5. Timeline

<Tip>

</Tip>

## Event Pipeline Column

Visual workflow showing events in different stages:

### Pipeline Stages

<Steps>
  <Step title="Considering">
    Events you've viewed but not yet tracked (from browsing history).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tracked">
    Events saved to your calendar. You plan to attend but haven't registered yet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Registered">
    Events you've officially signed up for. Registration confirmed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Attending">
    Events happening today or in the next 7 days.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Attended">
    Past events you marked as attended.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Drag-and-Drop

On desktop, you can drag event cards between pipeline stages to update status:

- Drag from "Tracked" to "Registered" after you sign up
- Drag from "Registered" to "Attended" after the event

<Tip>
  Pipeline management helps you stay organized and never miss registration deadlines.
</Tip>

## Tips & Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Mark Attendance Promptly">
    After attending an event, mark it as "Attended" within 24 hours. This keeps your dashboard accurate and helps TechCal improve recommendations.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Review Weekly">
    Set a reminder to check your dashboard every Monday. Review upcoming events, goal progress, and adjust your event tracking accordingly.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Set Realistic Goals">
    Aim for 1-2 events per week if you're employed full-time. Overcommitting leads to burnout and missed events.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Track Skills Learned">
    After each event, note which skills you practiced or learned. This data powers your Learning Progress card.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Use Pipeline to Stay Organized">
    Move events through pipeline stages as your plans solidify. This prevents last-minute scrambles for registration.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Goal progress not updating">
    **Cause**: Events aren't marked as attended, or event skills don't match your goal.

    **Solution**:
    1. Mark past events as "Attended"
    2. Ensure your profile goals are specific (e.g., "Learn React" not "Learn coding")
    3. Refresh dashboard data
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Dashboard is empty">
    **Cause**: You haven't tracked or attended any events yet.

    **Solution**: Visit the [Discovery page](/features/discovery) and track some events. Your dashboard will populate as you engage with events.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Learning Progress shows incorrect skills">
    **Cause**: Event tags or skills are mismatched with your profile.

    **Solution**: Update your profile skills in **Settings** → **Career Profile**. TechCal will re-calculate skill matches.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
