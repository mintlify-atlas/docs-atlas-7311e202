## Create Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up">
    Visit [techcal.com](https://techcal.com) and click **Sign Up** in the top right.

    You can sign up with:
    - **Email**: Create a password and verify your email
    - **Google**: One-click signup with your Google account
    - **GitHub**: Connect your GitHub profile

    <Note>
      Using Google or GitHub is fastest—no email verification required.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Your Profile">
    After signup, you'll see the **Career Onboarding** wizard. This 5-step process helps TechCal personalize your event recommendations.

    ### Step 1: Career Basics
    - **Current Role**: Your job title (e.g., "Software Engineer", "Product Manager")
    - **Seniority**: Junior, Mid-level, Senior, or Lead
    - **Industry**: Tech sector you work in (SaaS, FinTech, E-commerce, etc.)

    ### Step 2: Your Skills
    - **Primary Skills**: What you're already good at (e.g., Python, React, AWS)
    - **Skills to Learn**: What you want to improve or learn next

    <Tip>
      Add at least 3-5 skills in each category for the best recommendations.
    </Tip>

    ### Step 3: Career Goals
    Select your main objectives:
    - Learn new technologies
    - Advance to senior roles
    - Switch to a new field
    - Build your network
    - Find a new job

    ### Step 4: Event Preferences
    - **Budget**: How much you can spend on events (Free only, $0-100, $100-500, $500+, Unlimited)
    - **Format**: Virtual, In-person, or Both
    - **Event Types**: Conferences, hackathons, meetups, workshops, career fairs

    ### Step 5: Location & Timezone
    - Set your timezone for accurate event times
    - Add your city/country for location-based filtering

    <Warning>
      You can skip onboarding, but your recommendations won't be personalized. Complete it now for the best experience.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Explore the Discover Page">
    You'll land on the **Discover** page—your personalized event feed.

    ![Discover Workspace](/images/discover-workspace.png)

    Here you'll see:
    - **Event cards** with title, date, location, and format
    - **Career Impact Score** (0-100) showing relevance to your profile
    - **Quick filters** at the top (This Week, Free Events, Virtual Only, etc.)
    - **Advanced filters** in the left sidebar
  </Step>

  <Step title="Find Your First Event">
    Let's filter for a free, upcoming event:

    1. Click **Free Events** in the quick filter bar
    2. Scroll through results—events are sorted by Career Impact Score
    3. Click any event card to view details

    ### Understanding Event Details

    The event detail panel shows:
    - **Description**: What the event is about
    - **Speakers**: Who's presenting
    - **Agenda**: Schedule and sessions
    - **Career Impact Breakdown**: Why this event is relevant to you
    - **Who's Going**: Other TechCal users attending (if public profiles)

    <Tip>
      Look for the **Career Impact Explanation** to understand exactly how this event helps your career goals.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save to Calendar">
    Found an event you like? Save it to your calendar:

    1. In the event detail panel, click **Track Event**
    2. The event now appears in your **Calendar** tab and **Dashboard**
    3. You'll get reminders before registration deadlines

    ### Sync to External Calendar (Optional)

    To add events to Google Calendar or Outlook:

    1. Go to **Settings** → **Connect Calendar**
    2. Authorize TechCal to access your calendar
    3. All tracked events will sync automatically

    <Note>
      You can also export individual events as `.ics` files from the event detail view.
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tips for Getting the Most from TechCal

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Update Your Profile Regularly">
    As you learn new skills or your goals change, update your career profile in **Settings** → **Career Profile**. This keeps your recommendations fresh and relevant.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Use the 'Near Me' Filter">
    Click the **Near Me** quick filter to find local in-person events. TechCal uses your location to show nearby conferences and meetups.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Check 'Who's Going'">
    Before attending an event, check the **Who's Going** section to see if anyone from your network will be there. Great for planning meetups!
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Mark Attendance After Events">
    After attending an event, mark it as **Attended** in your dashboard. This:
    - Builds your career history
    - Improves future recommendations
    - Unlocks progress tracking features
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Explore Different Event Types">
    Don't just attend conferences. Try:
    - **Meetups**: Great for local networking
    - **Workshops**: Hands-on skill building
    - **Hackathons**: Portfolio projects and teamwork
    - **Career Fairs**: Job opportunities and company exposure
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Why am I seeing events outside my budget?">
    Make sure you've set your budget preference in your profile. Go to **Settings** → **Career Profile** → **Budget Preference** and select your range. Then apply the budget filter in the Discover sidebar.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do Career Impact Scores work?">
    Career Impact Scores (0-100) are calculated based on:
    - **Skill Relevance**: How well the event matches your skills and learning goals
    - **Career Stage Match**: Appropriate difficulty for your seniority
    - **Networking Value**: Opportunities to meet relevant people
    - **Industry Relevance**: Alignment with your target industry

    Higher scores mean the event is more relevant to YOUR specific career goals.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use TechCal without completing onboarding?">
    Yes, but you won't get personalized recommendations. Events will be sorted by popularity instead of career relevance. We strongly recommend completing your profile.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I change my timezone?">
    Go to **Settings** → **Profile** → **Timezone**. Event times will update automatically based on your selection.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
