## What is TechCal?

TechCal is an intelligent event discovery and career acceleration platform designed for tech professionals. We help you find conferences, hackathons, meetups, and workshops that align with your career goals, skills you want to learn, and networking objectives.

Instead of browsing endless event listings, TechCal learns what matters to your career and surfaces the most relevant opportunities—automatically.

## Key Benefits

### Personalized Event Recommendations

After completing your career profile, TechCal scores every event based on:
- **Skills alignment**: How well the event matches your current skills and learning goals
- **Career stage fit**: Appropriate difficulty level for your seniority
- **Networking value**: Opportunities to meet peers, mentors, or hiring managers
- **Industry relevance**: Alignment with your target industry or tech stack

You'll see a **Career Impact Score (0-100)** for each event with an explanation of why it's relevant to you.

### Smart Filtering & Search

Find exactly what you need with powerful filters:
- **Budget**: Free events only, paid tiers, or unlimited
- **Format**: Virtual, in-person, or hybrid
- **Location**: Near me, specific cities, or global virtual events
- **Date range**: This week, this month, or custom ranges
- **Category**: Conferences, hackathons, meetups, workshops, career fairs
- **Difficulty**: Beginner, intermediate, or advanced

### Calendar Integration

Sync events directly to Google Calendar, Outlook, or Apple Calendar. Never miss registration deadlines or event dates.

### Team Formation for Hackathons

Join or create teams for hackathons directly in TechCal. Find teammates with complementary skills and collaborate through built-in chat.

### Career Progress Tracking

Your dashboard shows:
- Events attended by category
- Skills you've learned from events
- Networking growth (people you've met)
- Career milestones reached
